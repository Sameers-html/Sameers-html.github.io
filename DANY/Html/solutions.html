HIVE is an e-commerce solution that helps in delivering products directly from the central warehouse
or micro fulfillment centers to the retailers.

How does HIVE work?
Hive focuses on 3 scopes to make the FMCGs and retailers achieve full productivity.
1- Digitizing retailer demand signal: we offer your retailer a digital solution through the Retail app to
    digitize the demand signal so whenever they want to buy your product, you can easily send it to them.
2- MFC/ stock management solution: we enable you to sell directly from your MFC to your retailer so that
    you can own your relationship with them.
3- Instant distribution service: previously your retailers had to wait from 3 days to a week to receive your
    product, but now we offer instant distribution delivery service to make the retailers even more loyal to
    your brand.
4- Credit management: we offer you a secure block chain system to manage your credit and digitize your
    invoicing system and it’s called 1Pay.
5- Loyalty: Do you want to offer your loyal retailers good discounts? We offer you through our loyaltysystem.
Hive is a DaaS  (e-distribution as a service)
