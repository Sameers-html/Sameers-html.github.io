1Trolley is an integrated end to end digital transformation partner for
key retailers and brand owners, providing mobile, Q-commerce,
last-mile delivery, and white-label solutions.

The 1Trolley platform drives the digitization of its partners from
demand generation to last-mile experience, enabling bullet delivery of
products & services.

“We are focused on empowering communities through digital transformation.
We take a unique approach in fully understanding the end to end supply chain and
address its key pain points.” 1Trolley CEO, Walid Rashwan.
